The Problem

Sound Familiar?

The concert was incredible. Getting home? Not so much.

The Surge

Uber and Lyft prices spike $50–$100+ the second the encore ends. You're trapped into paying or you're stranded.

The Search

You're squinting at your phone trying to find a silver Honda Civic in a sea of thousands of people. Your driver can't find you either.

The Dead Zone

Cell towers are overwhelmed by tens of thousands of simultaneous requests. No service means no ride. You're on your own.

The Dippi Way

Three Steps. Zero Stress.

Like an airport taxi line — but for concerts. Pre-register, show up, and go.

1

Pre-Register

Book your ride home when you buy your concert tickets. Flat rate. Up to 4 riders. Done in 30 seconds.

2

Show Your Pass

Flash your QR boarding pass at the Dippi pickup zone. Works completely offline — no cell service needed.

3

Hop In & Go

Get in the next available car. Driver already has your destination. 30 seconds to match. You're on your way.
